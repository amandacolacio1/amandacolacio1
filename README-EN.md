# 👩‍💻👋 Hi, I'm Amanda!

I'm a **Full Stack Developer** who has been working in the technology industry since **2021**, with experience building **web**, **mobile**, and **automation** solutions using **JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, React, React Native, Next.js, and Node.js**.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in **Information Systems** from **PUC Minas** (2023) and a postgraduate degree in **Full Stack Development** from the same university. 

🇧🇷 I'm currently based in **Contagem, Minas Gerais**, part of the **Belo Horizonte metropolitan area**, Brazil.

This GitHub is where I share my journey as a developer. Here you'll find projects that reflect my continuous learning, hands-on experience, and passion for building practical solutions. Each repository represents an opportunity to explore new technologies, strengthen my skills, and document my professional growth.

I believe that continuous learning is one of the most important aspects of software development, and I use this portfolio to showcase that evolution through real projects and experiments.

Feel free to explore my work. Welcome! 🚀
